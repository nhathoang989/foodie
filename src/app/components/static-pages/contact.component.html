Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 30-45 minutes, depending on your location and the restaurant's preparation time.

What are your delivery fees?

Delivery fees start at $2.99 and may vary based on distance and demand. Free delivery is available for orders over $25.

Can I track my order?

Yes! You'll receive real-time updates via SMS and email, plus you can track your order status in your account.

What if there's an issue with my order?

Contact us immediately through phone, email, or live chat. We'll resolve any issues and ensure you're satisfied.

Do you offer group ordering?

Absolutely! You can place large orders for offices, events, or groups. Contact us for special pricing and coordination.

How do I become a restaurant partner?

We'd love to work with you! Fill out our partnership form or email partnerships@foodieexpress.com for more information.
